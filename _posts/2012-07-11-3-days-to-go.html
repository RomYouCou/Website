As you may know if you follow our Twitter or Facebook Page we have announced the lineup. For those of you unaware or unsure of it the event is a free public music festival that promotes our local music talent. The event this year is to be held on Saturday 14th July 2012 between 12-6PM. Once again we have the lovely big stage booked, lots of brilliant music and some stalls from Naomi House who we are supporting again this year. Also joining us in the park will the the Romsey Arts Group. As you may be aware, the weather has been typical British weather (rain) however we are still hoping to go ahead with the festival. To keep updated with any changes or announcements please follow our Twitter and Facebook Page. We shall post/tweet at the very latest Friday (13th July 2012) at 1pm stating whether the event is definitely on/off. If you have any questions please contact us by Email (contact@ryc.org.uk) or if you know + have Ben Evans's phone number, use that.
